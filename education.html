My Education

If I recall correctly, I attended Tadika Ho in Seri Kembangan when I was 6 years old.

At 7, I was a student at Sekolah Rendah Kebangsaan Putrajaya Presint 9 (1). I stayed in SKPP9(1) until I took my UPSR at 12. When I turned 13, I simply moved to the high school next door, Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Putrajaya Presint 9 (1) and took both my PT3 and SPM there as well.

Currently, I am continuing my education at UiTM Rembau as a Diploma student.
